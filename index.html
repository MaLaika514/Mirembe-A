Code Queen refers to various initiatives and individuals dedicated to empowering women in the technology field, particularly in coding and web development.
One notable initiative is the Code Queen Program (Uganda), a free three-month training program founded in 2019 by Sonal Kadchha. The program is implemented by Educating the Children in partnership with The Innovation Village in Kampala, Uganda.
The initiative is designed to support unemployed young women from disadvantaged backgrounds by equipping them with both technical and professional skills. The training covers web development including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and databases as well as emerging areas such as artificial intelligence, alongside project management, digital marketing, public speaking, and essential life skills. The program is practical, industry responsive, and strongly focused on enhancing employability, enabling young women to confidently pursue careers in STEM fields.
